Design Clinic- School of Information

Design Associate // Oct. 2017 - Dec. 2017

« Back to projects Next Project › ‹ Previous Project


Urū sports is a global online platform matching field hockey athletes with teams that have unfilled playing positions. Our team worked on improving the user experience of the ‘Profile’ and ‘Search’ features. We started with evaluating usability of the current website through interaction map and heuristic evaluation. With the key findings, we brainstormed and created paper prototype. We did 4 usability tests and improved our design ideas based on users’ feedbacks. Finally, we came up with a high-fidelity prototype and a style guide that could be used by our client for future development. The overall experience is instructive, we learned how to apply knowledge acquired in class to solve problems in real world while accumulate practical skills through team collaboration and client communication.
