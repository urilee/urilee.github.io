Interaction Design Studio

Team Member // Sept. 2017 - Dec. 2017


Our team worked to develop a prototype of a mobile application to be used inline with the current University of Michigan Paratransit Service.



« Back to projects Next Project › ‹ Previous Project


The Problem:

Students are often left temporarily disabled unexpectedly; from injury or other factors. They are forced to quickly figure out how to live and be a student with barriers that they are not typically used to.

The current Paratransit service is only accessible over the phone, rides must be scheduled 24 hours beforehand, and students are unable to track the bus.



The Solution:

A mobile application which allows students to track their scheduled bus ride on a live maps system, and can notify the driver if they are running late in order to allow a 10 minute grace period. Students can also schedule rides also 24 hours on the application beforehand without calling.
